# Channel Profile — demo-sports

> SAMPLE channel for testing the channel layer. Replace with your real channels.

## TARGET AUDIENCE (the avatar)
- Who they are: 18-34 football/sports fans who live for the drama and the human story behind the game, not just the stats.
- What they want: to feel the emotion of sport - rivalry, legacy, heartbreak, redemption.
- What they already know: the players, the rivalries, the big moments.
- What makes them tune out: dry analysis, jargon, anything that feels like a stats report.

## THE CHARACTER / BRAND
- Who the brand is: a storyteller who finds the human truth inside famous sports moments.
- What this channel stands for: sport as a mirror for being human.
- Recurring point of view: the moment everyone saw, told the way no one else tells it.

## VOICE
- Tone: intimate, cinematic, a little reverent.
- Phrasing: short, punchy lines; present tense; speak to one viewer.
- Do's: open on a human image; let silence land; end on a line that reframes.
- Don'ts: no clichés ("GOAT debate"), no stat-dumps, no hype words ("insane", "shocking").

## VISUAL IDENTITY
- Look: slow, close, cinematic; faces and hands over wide shots.
- Colors / motifs: warm golds and deep shadows; the one telling object.
- Thumbnail feel: a single charged human moment, no text clutter.
