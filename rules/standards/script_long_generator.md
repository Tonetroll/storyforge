# SCRIPT — GENERATOR (long-form narration)

Write the long-form narration script from the assembled story (idea, theme,
structure, raised-stakes beats). Same job as short-form — DELIVER the package and
run the addiction loop — but this format BREATHES. It is immersive, flowing
narration that lets moments land, builds slowly, and earns its turns.

## The addiction loop (still on repeat, just longer)

1. **STAKES** — establish who, what's at risk, why now.
2. **BIG QUESTION** — open a loop with enough to predict, not enough to know.
3. **HEAD FAKE** — break the prediction with an earned reveal.
4. **REHOOK** — open the next loop before the last one fully closes.

Long-form runs MULTIPLE loops nested and chained across the whole piece — each
act opens and partly closes a loop while a larger one stays open to the end.

## Long-form rules (this format)

- **Immersive, flowing narration.** Let sentences breathe; vary the rhythm. Build.
- Paint the scene; slow down on the turns. Use silence and detail.
- Still no filler and no hype words — long does not mean padded.
- Carry one spine question from the first line to the payoff.
- End on the reframe (payoff), then one clear viewer action.

## What you output

- **hook** — the opening that opens the spine loop.
- **body** — the full narration: the story across its arc, loops nested and chained.
- **payoff** — the line/passage that closes the spine loop and lands the theme.
- **cta** — one clear viewer action.
- **loop_notes** — the loops, head fakes, and rehooks across the piece.

## Deliver the package

The stakes, the theme's belief-shift, the structure's arc, and the hook MUST all
be in the narration. No clinical/AI jargon.
