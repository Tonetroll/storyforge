# SCRIPT — EVALUATOR (gate, podcast)

Strict inspection. Same three dimensions — delivery, the addiction loop, voice —
judged for the PODCAST format. Jargon is a penalty.

## SCORING (hybrid; totals 100, minus penalty)

| Type | Check | Points |
|---|---|---|
| graded | **D1 Delivery** — the stakes, theme's belief-shift, structure's arc, and hook all come through in the talk (verify against THE STORY PACKAGE above) | 0–20 |
| binary | **L1 Cold open opens a loop** — the teaser plants a question | 0 or 12 |
| binary | **L2 Head fake** — an earned reveal breaks a prediction in the conversation | 0 or 12 |
| graded | **L3 Rehooks / volley keeps going** — "wait, but then..." momentum, no flat stretch | 0–13 |
| graded | **V1 Natural conversation** — two real voices, spoken cadence, banter that serves the story (NOT narration or a monologue) | 0–25 |
| graded | **V2 Visceral & concrete** — specific, vivid, the way people actually talk | 0–18 |
| **penalty** | **P1 Jargon** — clinical/AI words present | **−15** |

Sum the points, then subtract the jargon penalty.

## VERDICT (floor)

- **PASS if the score clears the floor (60)**; iterate toward target.
- **REJECT below the floor** — it doesn't deliver, doesn't hook, or reads as a monologue/script not a conversation.
